Location Data

When granted permission, the Mobile app will collect data about your location when the app is being in use. The app uses the data collected to:

  • Calculate the price of your trip by calculating the distance from your location to destination;
  • The app also uses your location to match you with the closest available rider to you;
  • To receive delivery and ride requests based on your location;
  • To help drivers for navigation during a trip

This information will be used by Google Maps API to achieve the above listed. Your information will be kept private and secured.
